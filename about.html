About

Providing Objective Information

Laois Political believes in open and transparent political representation, and aims to provide objective and transparent information to inform Laois voters about the performance of their elected representatives. We do this by compiling statistics from County Council Chamber Meetings, Budgetry and other votes as well as County Councillors' annuak expense claims.

Ultimately, our mission is to connect our communities more fully with political representation and to make voting in local elections a more inclusive and informed process.

A Platform for Engagement

Not only does Laois Political provide information about the County Councillors' performance, but it also aims to act as a platform for political engagement and debate.

We do this by providing a facility for members of the Laois community, along with local political representatives, to contribute articles to the site about issues that are important to them.
